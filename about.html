Who Am I?

I am a mobile developer with 1+ year(s) of experience on both iOS & Android platforms. I am looking to make a career out of it. For me, this is a very exciting field to be in because as the technology advances, many opportunities arise for this field. In particular, I decided to go into the world of mobile development because I realized that these devices are ubiquitous, becoming more and more powerful each day. Today, whatever we can do on a computer, we can probably do on a mobile device. This is what I am looking forward to, since now, we are able to integrate many exciting technologies into our mobile devices, for example: machine and deep learning with mobile apps.

I've mostly been working on developing apps for both iOS & Android. I have developed a couple of apps while working for Rauland, a Division of AMETEK, Inc. company. With a team of 3 more people, we've developed an Android app, which can be used by patients inside hospitals, and nursing facilities to communicate with their caregiver. We've used different technologies for this app. For the back-end we have used Spring Framework, MSSQL for storing our data, while for the front-end we used Android (Java). Of the libraries for Android, we've used GSON to serialize/deserialize JSON data, Retrofit, OkHttp to send requests and get responses from our server. - Another app on which I have been working on, also for Rauland, is an app for hospital staff, which can be used to receive calls sent by patients from the patient app. I've used Xamarin (C#) to develop a cross-platform app, available for both iOS & Android at the same time. - Currently, I'm working on an iOS app which communicates via SIP to our server. I've been using PortSIP VoIP SDK in order to register to the SIP server and make/receive audio/video calls.
